About Us

Welcome to PolitePost, the AI-powered communication assistant designed for the modern workplace.

Our Mission

We believe that professional communication shouldn't be stressful. In a high-pressure corporate environment, it is easy to let frustration slip into your emails. A single "rude" message can damage a reputation you took years to build.

Our mission is simple: To protect your career by ensuring your written communication is always diplomatic, professional, and clear.

How It Works

PolitePost utilizes advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) to analyze the sentiment of your text. It identifies aggression, slang, and informal phrasing, then reconstructs the message using standard corporate etiquetteâ€”without losing your original meaning.

Our Privacy Commitment

We understand that you may be drafting sensitive work emails. That is why we operate on a strictly anonymous basis. We do not require a login, and we do not store, log, or read your emails. Once your text is processed, it is erased from our system instantly.
