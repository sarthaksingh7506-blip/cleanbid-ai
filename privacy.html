Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

1. Data Minimization (No Storage)

PolitePost operates on a strict "No Storage" policy regarding your input data. When you type text into our tool:

  • The text is sent to our AI processor (Groq/Llama 3) via a secure, encrypted connection.
  • The AI processes the text to generate a rewriting suggestion.
  • Once the response is returned to your browser, the text is immediately discarded from the server memory.
  • We do not save, log, or train models on your emails.

2. Cookies & Advertising

We use third-party vendors, including Google AdSense, to serve ads to keep this tool free.

  • Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

3. Analytics

We may use anonymous analytics (like Cloudflare Web Analytics) to count the number of visitors to our site. This data is aggregate and does not identify individual users or link to the text you type.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact us via the contact details provided on our GitHub repository or domain registrar.
